Sharp.idx Glossary

Scores
SI Score Sharp Index score (0–100). Combines three signals into one number. 70+ = worth watching, 80+ = strong signal.
RLM (10%) Reverse Line Movement. Line moved against where public money is going — a sign sharp bettors pushed it the other way. Weighted lower because it requires opening line history to be accurate.
PINNACLE (52%) The gap between Pinnacle's devigged price and the average soft book price. Pinnacle accepts sharp bettors and prices more accurately — a large gap means they see the side differently from recreational books. Highest weight because it's pure price data.
MONEY LAYER (38%) Exchange and velocity signals. Gets boosted by Novig/ProphetX exchange confirmation, line velocity (how fast it moved recently), and soft book divergence (how much books disagree with each other).
pp (gap) Percentage points — the gap between Pinnacle's fair implied probability and the soft book average. A 3pp gap means Pinnacle prices this side 3% more likely than the market average.
Signal Types
Vegas Backed Pinnacle (and/or exchanges) pricing this side meaningfully higher than recreational books. The sharpest, most reliable signal.
Dual Consensus Both Pinnacle AND an exchange (Novig or ProphetX) confirm the sharp side at 1.5pp+. Two independent markets agreeing — strongest possible signal.
Pressure Move Line moved against where the public is betting (Reverse Line Movement detected). Books are accepting sharp action on the unpopular side.
Exchange Signal Novig or ProphetX (zero-vig exchanges) pricing this side away from the soft book market. Exchange prices reflect pure market consensus.
Counter-Public The sharp signal is on the side the public is NOT betting. Public typically backs favorites, overs, and home teams — going the other way with sharp backing is a strong combination.
Badges
🔒 Line Freeze The line hasn't moved from opening despite heavy public pressure on the other side. Books are comfortable letting sharp money ride — they don't need to move the line because they want action on the non-public side.
⚡ Steam Pinnacle's line moved 5+ points in the last ~15 minutes — possible large sharp bet or syndicate action.
⚡ Confirmed Steam Both Pinnacle AND the soft book average moved the same direction simultaneously. Much stronger signal — multiple books reacting to the same sharp action.
Novig ✓ Novig exchange (zero-vig, pure market pricing) confirms the Pinnacle side. When both agree, it's two independent sharp markets pointing the same direction.
My Plays
CLV ▲/▼ Closing Line Value. ▲ = you entered at a better price than opening (positive CLV — you were ahead of the market). ▼ = you entered worse than opening (negative CLV — market had already moved). Positive CLV over time = good signal identification.
Lead Signal Which pillar drove the SI score on this play — Pinnacle-led, RLM-led, Money-led, or Multi-signal (two or more aligned). Over time this shows which signal types are actually predictive for your bets.
